    About Mister Paver

    Ramon started in the landscaping since he was very young. By that time, by personal desire, he renovated the patio of his parents' house. Since then, he became passionate about the landscaping industry.

    Ramon is known by his high quality and detail oriented. His main goal is to achieve customer satisfaction.

    For over 14 years he has been in the landscaping industry, but it was until 2019 when he founded Mister Paver and he has used his knowledge and passion to build beautiful and functional landscapes in Vancouver.

    Also, he has built couple projects designed by the prestigious architect Paul Sangha, one of the best landscape arquitects in North America.
